# Adoption & Training Plan

## 1. Rollout Strategy
Phased approach to minimize risk and maximize feedback loops.

### Phase 1: Pilot (Alpha)
- **Audience:** Core project team + 10 friendly users (Super Users).
- **Goal:** Validate core workflows and catch "showstopper" bugs.
- **Support:** White-glove, direct Slack channel.

### Phase 2: Early Adopters (Beta)
- **Audience:** One full department or regional office (~50-100 users).
- **Goal:** Test scale, support processes, and training materials.
- **Activities:** Town hall demo, daily office hours.

### Phase 3: General Availability (Go-Live)
- **Audience:** Entire organization.
- **Goal:** Full adoption.
- **Activities:** Company-wide launch email, executive sponsorship video, swag drops.

## 2. Champions Program
Leveraging power users to drive peer-to-peer adoption.

- **Selection:** 1 Champion per ~20 users or per major team.
- **Benefits:** Early access to features, "Certified Expert" badge, direct line to Product Ops.
- **Responsibilities:**
    - Answer basic peer questions (Tier 0 support).
    - Relay feedback/pain points to Ops team.
    - Host "Lunch & Learn" sessions for their team.

## 3. Role-Based Training Curriculum

### Pathway A: Employee (Basic User)
- **Format:** Micro-learning videos (LMS) + In-app walkthroughs (Nova).
- **Modules:**
    - Logging in & Profile Setup
    - Core Task Execution
    - Finding Help / Knowledge Base
- **Certification:** None required.

### Pathway B: Manager
- **Format:** Live webinar + Interactive PDF guide.
- **Modules:**
    - Everything in Pathway A
    - Approvals & Review Dashboards
    - Team Analytics
- **Certification:** "Manager Ready" quiz.

### Pathway C: Platform Admin
- **Format:** Hands-on workshop (Shadowing).
- **Modules:**
    - User Provisioning
    - Configuration Management
    - Security & Compliance
- **Certification:** Practical exam (sandbox environment).

## 4. Office Hours & Ongoing Enablement
- **Weekly Office Hours:** Open Zoom link for Q&A (Wednesdays 2 PM).
- **Monthly "What's New":** Newsletter highlighting new features and tips.
- **Q&A Bot:** "Nova" programmed with top 50 FAQs for instant answers.
